About Me

Behind the Lens

I'm Hareesh Rajapaksha, a photographer and content creator based in Hobart, Tasmania. Through RoyalRay Photography, I help people and brands tell their story with honesty and beauty.

My work spans couples and wedding photography, lifestyle portraits, and commercial content creation for local businesses — restaurants, retail, real estate, and more. Every shoot is thoughtfully crafted to feel authentic, not staged.

When I'm not behind the camera, I'm building creative concepts, editing, and chasing the right light across Tasmania's extraordinary landscape.
